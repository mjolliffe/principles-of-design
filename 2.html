DIETER RAMS | 10 PRINCIPLES FOR GOOD DESIGN

GOOD DESIGN IS...

USEFUL

USEFUL
Good design makes a product useful. A product is bought to be used.
It has to satisfy not only functional, but also psychological and aesthetic criteria.
Good design emphasizes the usefulness of a product
whilst disregarding anything that could detract from it.
