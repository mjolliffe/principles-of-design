DIETER RAMS: PRINCIPLES FOR GOOD DESIGN

GOOD DESIGN IS...

6

HONEST
Good design does not make a product appear more innovative, powerful or valuable than it really is. It does not attempt to manipulate the consumer with promises that cannot be kept.
